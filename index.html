Causes of Social Media Addiction

Social media platforms are designed to be addictive using mechanisms like instant gratification and fear of missing out.

Consequences of Addiction

Resources and Solutions

Practical tips for reducing social media addiction:

  • Set daily limits for social media usage.
  • Use apps to track and restrict time spent on platforms.
  • Engage in offline activities like exercise or hobbies.
